@dvshkn@mastodon.technology I started with a Fresh Roast iRoast2, which is a little 5oz air roaster. It was almost too much trouble for so little coffee.

The Behmor 1600 drum roaster I have now was twice the price but does 1/2 lb and has a lot more automation than the Fresh Roast. I'd say jump straight to that if you can afford it and the hobby appeals.

Otherwise, there's always just a pan and a source of heat and see what happens :)
