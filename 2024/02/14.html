LRT: "atproto is built on the assumption that anybody should be able to index the network"

I think that's a key thing to understand about Bluesky's flavor of decentralization. Mastodon and ActivityPub aren't shaped like that. Some folks agree the whole federated network should be indexable and discoverable - and some folks really, really don't.

Folks in the former camp may find a lot to like about Bluesky & atproto. From the latter camp, I think there will always be vociferous resistance.

It's kinda but not really like having an open-plan house versus a house with many little rooms. I think folks who favor one over the other are baffled by the opposite opinion.
