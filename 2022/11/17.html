How I acquired my stash of Rush CDs in high school still makes me giggle

I had a comic that was, I think, the first appearance of Cable in New Mutants no. 87. My friend desperately wanted it. He was also an enormous fan of Rush. Owned every album.

One day he was like, "What will it take to get that comic from you?"

And I was like, "Gimme all your Rush CDs," not thinking he'd ever do that.

But, he was like, "Deal. They're all in my trunk right now. Gimme the comic."

So I gave him the comic and he gave me like 15 CDs. I guess the comic's worth $150 now? I dunno, I still listen to the CDs occasionally though.
