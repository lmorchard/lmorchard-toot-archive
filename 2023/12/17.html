@evan @simon @luis_in_brief I raise that sort of thing with probably annoying frequency:

I think we're used to thinking of "portability" as moving your stuff from one neutral fungible service provider to another. Like from AWS to GCP, which probably have very similar usage policies.

But, on the fediverse in particular, instance admins are often strongly opinionated and have distinct policies.

So, you're not just "moving your stuff", you're asking a new admin to accept a history of posting that hasn't gone through their local notions of moderation.
