So, a few months ago, I was belaboring over whether to use Wasabi or Backblaze as a remote backup service from my Synology NAS. Both seem well supported out of the box.

Ended up going with Backblaze B2, since I couldn't seem to get good pricing info out of Wasabi without scheduling a sales call.

Backblaze, at least, seemed straightforward: I figured they would charge me around $6-10 per month for just the essentials backed up from my NAS.

Turns out I didn't account for the free service thresholds: so far they've only charged me $2 per month for my tentative hunk of backup less than 1TB.

This pleases me greatly.
